Model Overview
Methodology
1 · Proportional Devigging
Each market (Win, Top 3, Top 5, Top 10) is devigged independently using the proportional method. The field overround is calculated from the sum of implied probabilities against the expected sum (1, 3, 5, 10 for each market).
2 · Finish Distribution Fitting
A Generalised Gamma distribution is fitted to the devigged fair probabilities for positions 1–10, exactly hitting all four cumulative market anchors. The tail (positions 11+) is an independent unimodal distribution with a slope-continuous join at position 10.
3 · Two-Stage Simulation
1,000,000 full-field races are run. For each race each driver draws from their fitted distribution. Drivers who finish in the top-10 zone use their sampled GG position. When both drivers finish outside top-10, the matchup is resolved using their devigged LS ratio — guaranteeing market-implied separation in the dominant tail scenario.
4 · Output
Fair probabilities have zero additional vig. Your vig input applies a margin on top of the fair price. Both fair and priced odds are shown in decimal and American format.
